About Marilyn M. Jones & Associates

Marilyn M. Jones & Associates are court reporters based out of Michigan City, Indiana. Their court reporting services is professional, dependable, and certified with over 30 years of experience.

About the Project

Marilyn M. Jones & Associates old website was outdated and created with the use of tables for its design. They wanted to showcase a Michigan City lighthouse to be emphasis on their new site.

This new design was developed from WordPress and gave the site a new look without the use of tables. The Michigan City lighthouse was placed in the background of the header, so it could be displayed on all pages, including the mobile version.
