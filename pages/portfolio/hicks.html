About Law Office of John Taylor Hicks

Attorney John Taylor Hicks is an Seattle criminal defense attorney who works tirelessly for his clients since 1985.

About the Project

The Law Office of John Taylor Hicks wanted to create a new website to showcase himself, his personal quote, and the city of Seattle. He also request a section be added for his peer endorsements.

With the use of CSS, his photo, personal quote, and information were placed on the top in the header section for his new website. The header also included the Seattle landscape in the background. On the right side column, under the contact form, quotes from his peer endorsements were added including a separate page where all peer endorsements could be read.
