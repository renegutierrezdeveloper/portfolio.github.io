About Cummings & Cummings

Cummings & Cummings is a law firm based out of Fort Worth, Texas. They are criminal defense lawyers with over 27 years of experience.

About the Project

Cummings & Cummings' old website was a basic template and outdated. It was in need of a refreshed style and a modern design. His brand was a central point for his new website.

The new site was developed to showcase Attorney Fred Cummings' logo and an image of himself. It also focused on his where he practiced law and that he specialized in criminal defense law to promote his brand. The new design gave the website a more modern style.
