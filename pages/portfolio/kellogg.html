About the Law Offices of Michael L. Kellogg, Inc.

Law Offices of Michael L. Kellogg, Inc. is a Riverside County law firm based out of California. This law firm deals with variety of legal issues. Attorney Kellogg has been an practicing law for over 20 years.

About the Project

Attorney Michael Kellogg wanted to create a new website focused on bankruptcy, criminal defense, personal injury, and DUI.

The new website included separate pages for each legal issue. Images on the homepage represented each area of law and each image linked to its corresponding page. The new design is fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and smartphone devices.
