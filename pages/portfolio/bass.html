About Law Office of Caroline Bass

Law Office of Caroline Bass was a law firm based out of Santa Fe, New Mexico specializing in a wide of range of Family Law issues with over 23 years of experience.

About the Project

Law Office of Caroline Bass needed help redesigning her website. She wanted an updated and fresh look for her website. On the homepage, she also wanted a photo of herself as the focus point on desktop platforms.

I made sure the website showcased an image of the attorney when users first visited the site. The new website was responsive and the image of herself would disappear on tablets and mobile platforms.
